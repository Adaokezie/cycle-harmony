How to Relieve Menstrual Cramps

Menstrual cramps can be a painful part of your period, but there are several methods to help relieve them. Here are a few tips:

  • Apply heat to your lower abdomen using a heating pad or hot water bottle.
  • Exercise regularly to help reduce the severity of cramps.
  • Stay hydrated and avoid caffeine and salty foods.
  • Consider over-the-counter pain relievers like ibuprofen or acetaminophen.

Healthy Eating During Your Period

Eating a balanced diet can help manage symptoms of your period. Here are some foods to include:

  • Leafy green vegetables like spinach and kale for iron.
  • Fruits rich in water, such as watermelon and cucumber, to stay hydrated.
  • Lean proteins like chicken and fish to maintain energy levels.
  • Whole grains like oats and quinoa for sustained energy.
